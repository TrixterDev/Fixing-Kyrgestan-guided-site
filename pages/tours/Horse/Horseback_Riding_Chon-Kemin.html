Amazing lakes of Kyrgyzstan, 15 days

  • Itinerary
  • Price
  • Booking terms
  • Book now

Duration:8 days/7 nights

Type:Horseback riding tour

Best Season:late June – mid September

Accommodation:Hotels, Guesthouses, Yurt Camp, Tent.

Description :An adventures horse riding tour with long camping trail through the hidden valley and open meadows in the foothills of Tien-Shan Mountains. For many years, horses played a great role for nomads and now you can ride them in our Tien Shan Mountains. During this tour, you will ride a horse from Chon-Kemin Gorge to the mountain lake “Son-Kul” 3016m. You will meet shepherds and nomad is on the trail and can enjoy unique, untouched nature, mountains, lake, Mountain Rivers, forests of fir-trees.

Itinerary :Bishkek - Toktogul water reservoir – Sary Chelek Lake – Jalal-Abad – Kazarman – Naryn – KelSuu Lake – Tash Rabat - SonKol lake – Issyk-Kol lake – Karakol – Cholpon-Ata - Bishkek

Program:


Day Itinerary: Meals
1 Airport – Bishkek(40 km 40 min)
Arrival in Bishkek. Meeting at the airport “Manas”, transfer to the hotel. Rest. After lunch in local restaurant Bishkek sightseeing tour: visit Ala-Too square, Oak tree Park, Osh bazar (open market), Open Art Gallery, Opera and Ballet Theatre, Art Museum, History museum and etc. Dinner in local restaurant. Overnight in a hotel. 		B
2 Bishkek – Chon Kemin Nature Park –Kel-Kogur Lake (120 km, 2 hrs.+5-6hrs horse riding).
Breakfast. Transfer to Chon Kemin Nature Park for horseback riding. Meet with horseman and horses, load all necessary stuffs and start horseback riding to Kel-Kogur lake (2000m). Lunch-picnic on the way. Then continue ride through amazing landscape. Dinner and accommodation in tent near the lake. 		BLD
3 Kel-Kogur Lake -Chon Kemin Nature Park(20 km horse riding 5-6hrs)
Breakfast, we start our drive to Sary Chelek Lake (250 km 5-6 hrs) by highway. This alpine lake is the highlight of a larger area called Sary Chelek Nature Reserve, which has been declared as a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve in 1978, situated at the altitude of 1887 m and created by a landslide, the lake covers almost 500 hectares. The depth varies across the lake, with its deepest point at 245 meters. Lunch-picnic. Dinner and overnight in Family Home Stay. 		BLD
4 Breakfast. Back on the horse for the return trip. You will have again a very nice ride from rocky path to large and amazing panoramas on the lake all along the road. Dinner and overnight in Guesthouse.
Breakfast. Then we will take a walk to the Lake and hike up to the observation point. We will spend our whole day in Sary-Chelek reserve and enjoy the view and beauty of nature. This day we will be part of the nature. Lunch on the shore of the lake accompanied with snacks. Dinner and overnight in a guesthouse. 		BLD
5 Kelimchi valley – Son-Kul Lake (5-6 hrs.horse riding)
After breakfast riding to Jalgyz Karagai pass (3400 m), over the Song-Kol Mountains and into the lake’s basin. The morning climb affords wonderful views of Kilemche jailoo, and the pass itself is rocky and exciting. After lunch at Jaman Echki, follow the lakeshore east to the yurt at Batai Aral. Upon arrival, meet your host family of Kyrgyz shepherds in Son-Kul lake. Free time. Dinner and overnight are in a yurt camp. 		BLD
6 Son-kol lake - Tamchy(200km,5 hrs)
Breakfast. Transfer to Tamchy village, northern shore of Issyk-Kul lake via Kochkor.On the way visit handicraft and souvenir shop in Kochkor. Lunch in Kochkor in family guesthouse. Then continue drive to Tamchy. Issyk-Kul Lake along the North side of the celestial Issyk Kul Lake, the second largest alpine lake in the world (1608 m). Rest on the beach. Dinner and overnight in a guesthouse. 		BLD
7 Tamchy –Bishkek (230km,3,5 hrs.)
Breakfast. Rest on the beach. Transfer to Bishkek. Check-in to a hotel. Rest. Last moment shopping. Dinner in local restaurant. Overnight in a hotel. 		BLD
8 Bishkek- Airport(35km,40 min)
Bishkek- Airport(35km,40 min) ` 		BLD
9 Kel-Suu Lake excursion )
Breakfast. This day we hike to Kel-Suu Lake, 4-5 hrs. Optional: rent a horse and do horseback riding towards Lake or reach it by walking. Lunch-picnic near the lake. Afternoon back to Yurt Camp. Dinner and overnight in yurt. 		BLD
10 Kel Suu Lake – Son-Kol Lake (300 km 5-6hrs)
After breakfast, transfer to Son-Kol Lake (300 km, 5-6 hrs). Son-Kol Lake - high mountain lake at the altitude of 3060m, where you can see nomadic life, ancient culture, tradition and customs of Kyrgyz nomad is which is still alive today. Taste Kyrgyz national drink “Kymyz” (fermented mare’s milk). See edelweiss flower, golden eagle, camel, yaks and taste Kyrgyz national local nomad’s food. Lunch in Naryn town. You can take a walk near the lake or horse riding as option (not including package). Dinner and overnight in yurt. 		BLD
11 SonKol Lake – Bokonbaevo village (250 km, 5-6 hrs)
Breakfast. Drive to Bokonbaevo village (250 km, 5-6 hrs). On the way lunch in Kochkor village in Kyrgyz family. Issyk-Kul Lake, the second largest high-altitude alpine lake in the world after Titicaca Lake in South America. Locals called this lake “a pearl of Kyrgyzstan” and symbol of Kyrgyzstan. Dinner and overnight in guesthouse. 		BLD
12 Bokonbaevo village - Karakol (250 km, 4-5 hrs)
Breakfast. Transfer to Karakol (250 km, 4-5 hrs). On the way, visit Skaska Canyon (Fairy Tale). The whole originality of the place is the frozen in bizarre forms colorful red rocks, like after the eruption. Then we will visit Barskoon gorge known for its waterfalls and the Suok pass (4008m) from where you may get to the glaciers and high snow mountains. Check-in to hotel and rest. Overnight in hotel. 		B - -
13 Karakol - Cholpon-Ata (145 km)
Breakfast. Karakol sightseeing: - wooden Russian Orthodox Church, Dungan Mosque (XIX century), local bazaar. Then drive to Cholpon-Ata. Upon arrival lunch in local restaurant in Cholpon-Ata. Afternoon, you will visit Rukh Ordo, a unique cultural and spiritual complex of culture and tolerance of religious diversity. Located right on the shore of the lake it perfectly demonstrates its beauty and magnificence. Then, we will take a short break to give you the opportunity to visit ancient stone inscriptions (dating back to 800 B.C.). Overnight at the resort near the beach. 		B - -
14 Cholpon-Ata - Bishkek (280 km)
Breakfast. Free time on the beach. Transfer to Bishkek (280 km, 3-4 hrs) by highway. Lunch in Balykchy (Fisher village). Afternoon arrives in Bishkek. Check-in to hotel. Rest. Last moment shopping. Overnight in hotel. 		B - -
15 Bishkek - Airport (40 km)
Breakfast. Transfer to Airport. Departure. 		B
