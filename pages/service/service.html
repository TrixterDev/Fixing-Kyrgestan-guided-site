Our Transports

One of the main specialty of Kyrgyzstan is Mountains, about 94 % of the territory are Mountains with high passes, almost everywhere You look – will be facing into a new alpine vista or nomadic pastures. So, a great way to experience the Kyrgyzland and Nomads is – Travel by comfortable transport, such as:
- Jeep 4x4 (good for 3-4 people or Family Trip)
- Minibus Sprinter (great choice for a group or Collective of friends not more than 15 people)
- Bus/Couch (Ideal for a Cultural Tours with a group of 45 people)
- Soviet Trucks (perfect for Expeditions or Trekking transfers to reach the highest points of your destination with heavy supplements)
