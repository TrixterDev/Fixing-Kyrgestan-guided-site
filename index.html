Kyrgyz Guided Tours Team

Collective of creative professional guides, tour managers and inspired locals working in a partnership with local hosts and people, developing and bringing more adventures in tourism industry of Kyrgyzstan. We are fast growing young company, who are passionate on creating more adventures, making a bet on the quality of services and safety in traveling along the great silk road, cultural exploration and exchange.

More about us

We specialize in organizing group & individual tours

We specialize in organizing group & individual tours in the country of nomads — Kyrgyzstan and neighboring countries, which on the whole unite the territory called Central Asia. And our company can offer its customers a wide range of routes and programs:

  • - Silk Road Tours
  • - Cultural tours
  • - Expeditions
  • - Horse Riding Tours
  • - Trekking
  • - Mountain Biking
  • - Mountaineering
  • - Skiing & Snowboarding

Our passion and profession is to guide you through the Kyrgyzstan and Along the Great Silk Road world. More services you can get by sending us request letter or contact us by phone. Travelling with us you can get high-quality service, flexible prices, interesting offers, and special discounts.
